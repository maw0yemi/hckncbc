Hacker not cyber criminal

Hacker

This type of hacker are know as Ethical Hacker.An indivdual who has knownalge and ability to break into systems but only does this under a contract. The main goal of the hacker is to find, exploit and report any vulnerabilty found within the scope what was stated in the contract.

Some ethical hackers dispose of certifications such as CEH, OSCP, eJPT, etc. They also follow all laws, and cyber practice to ensure networks, companies, and personal data are save.

Cyber Criminal

A cybercriminal on the other hand is and idividual who has more o less the same skills and hacker but only exploit vulnerabilty for is own benefit without reporting to the company to fix the issue.

Instead of following laws they break them, they steal usernames, passwords, emial, addresses, bandalise and etc.
